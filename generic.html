Who am I?

A data explorer on a mission to turn messy numbers into clear stories. I’m a Data Analyst & Power BI Specialist who loves dashboards, automation, and uncovering insights hidden in spreadsheets. My goal? Help businesses see the story their data is trying to tell—one chart at a time.

I’m a Data Analyst and Microsoft Power BI Specialist with over 3 years of experience turning raw, complex datasets into actionable insights. I’ve worked across analytics and construction industries, designing dashboards, automating reporting, and optimizing database workflows using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. My passion lies in making data understandable and impactful—transforming numbers into stories that drive better business decisions. From cleaning historical data to building interactive dashboards with real-time updates, I focus on solutions that enhance efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making.

Beyond technical skills, I enjoy bridging the gap between business needs and data solutions, helping teams interpret insights and implement strategies effectively. I also take pride in mentoring colleagues and sharing best practices in analytics and reporting. Ultimately, my mission is to help organizations unlock the full potential of their data, enabling smarter strategies, improved performance, and meaningful growth.
